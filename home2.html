Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows (2011)

Synopsis

Director: Guy Ritchie

Genre: Mystery

Starring: Robert Downey Jr., Jude Law, Noomi Rapace, Jared Harris

Duration: 2h 8m

The film is set in 1891, where the brilliant yet eccentric detective Sherlock Holmes (Robert Downey Jr.) is investigating a series of seemingly random bombings and assassinations across Europe. Holmes quickly deduces that these isolated incidents are actually part of an elaborate, overarching conspiracy orchestrated by his intellectual equal and arch-nemesis, Professor James Moriarty (Jared Harris).


The Intellectual Chess Match

The story escalates as Dr. John Watson (Jude Law) prepares to marry Mary Morstan and settle down. Moriarty, viewing Holmes's interference as a threat, retaliates by targeting the newlyweds on their honeymoon train. Holmes barely manages to save them, forcing Watson to abandon his new life and join the investigation.


Their search for Moriarty leads them to Madam Simza Heron (Noomi Rapace), a mysterious Romani fortune teller whose missing brother, René, is inadvertently connected to Moriarty’s grand scheme. The trio must travel across the continent—from London to Paris, and through Germany—pursuing Moriarty as he executes his plan to gain control of the global arms trade and trigger a major European war for massive financial gain.

The Final Problem at Reichenbach


The high-stakes game of shadows culminates in a peace summit held at a remote fortress overlooking the Reichenbach Falls in Switzerland. Holmes knows this is where Moriarty intends to carry out his final, catastrophic act of assassination to ensure war erupts.


In a dramatic final confrontation, Holmes and Moriarty engage in a deadly chess match on a balcony. Knowing he is physically outmatched, Holmes makes one final, decisive move: he locks Moriarty in a bear hug and drags them both over the railing, sending them plunging into the roaring waters below.


The film concludes with Watson mourning Holmes, only to discover a subtle clue suggesting that the great detective may have, once again, outsmarted death itself

Meet The Players

Discover the cast that ignited the Victorian action and mystery in the first film.

Robert Downey Jr.

as Sherlock Holmes

The brilliant but eccentric Consulting Detective. He utilizes deduction and disguise to stop Moriarty's plot to ignite a world war.

Jude Law

as Dr. John Watson

Holmes's loyal partner and former military doctor. He is the Chronicler of the cases and the grounded counterpoint to Holmes's wild ideas.

Noomi Rapace

as Madam Simza Heron

A mysterious Romani Gypsy who is a key link in the investigation, as her missing brother is unwittingly involved in Moriarty's conspiracy.

Jarred Harris

Professor James Moriarty

The Arch-Nemesis. A highly intelligent and ruthless criminal mastermind and academic who orchestrates international terrorist attacks for financial gain.

Stephen Fry

Mycroft Holmes

Sherlock's older brother and a powerful Government Official. He secretly assists Sherlock, providing high-level access and protection when needed.
