Sherlock Holmes (2009)

Synopsis

Sherlock Holmes (2009)

Director: Guy Ritchie

Genre: Mystery

Starring: Robert Downey Jr., Jude Law, Rachel McAdams, Mark Strong

Duration: 2h 8m

The film transports us to the heart of London in the 1890s, reintroducing the world's most brilliant detective, Sherlock Holmes (portrayed by Robert Downey Jr.), and his loyal companion, Dr. John Watson (Jude Law). However, the peace in London does not last long. Shortly after his execution, Blackwood's grave is found open and empty, and reports surface that Blackwood has risen from the dead. Public belief in the occult begins to rise, causing chaos across the city. Holmes, who always relies on logic and science rather than superstition, rejects the idea of a supernatural resurrection. Joined by Watson, who is preparing to leave their life of adventure for his impending marriage, Holmes must investigate the case. Holmes' investigation mixes deduction, action, and unexpected alliances as he confronts a clever and dangerous adversary.
